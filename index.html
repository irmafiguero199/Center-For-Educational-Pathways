Our Journey in the Bronx

The Center For Educational Pathways (CEP) was established with a singular vision: to ensure that zip code does not determine destiny. Based in the heart of the Bronx, we provide a structured environment where students can explore their academic and professional interests.

Our foundation specializes in creating customized "pathways" for youth, ranging from STEM and coding to creative arts and leadership. We believe that by providing the right tools at the right time, we can transform the trajectory of an entire generation.

As a registered non-profit (EIN 83-0345654), we operate with complete transparency and a deep-rooted commitment to our community.

Our Mission & Core Values

We don't just teach; we inspire a lifelong passion for discovery and growth.

Inclusive Excellence

Mentorship First

Community Impact

Our success is measured by the growth of our community. We partner with local schools and businesses to create a supportive ecosystem for our students.

Transparency & Compliance

Privacy Policy

The Center For Educational Pathways (CEP) is committed to protecting student and donor privacy. We adhere to all state and federal data protection regulations. We never share or sell personal information to third parties, ensuring a safe digital environment for our mission.

Legal Status

CEP is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. Our EIN 83-0345654 is registered in the state of New York. All contributions are tax-deductible to the full extent of the law. We maintain open financial records to ensure accountability.
