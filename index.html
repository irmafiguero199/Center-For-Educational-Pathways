Institutional Mission

Empowering Communities Through Innovative Education

The Center for Educational Pathways is a registered non-profit organization located in the Bronx, New York. Our primary mission is to provide accessible, high-quality educational resources and specialized training programs designed to bridge the gap between academic theory and professional practice.

We focus on creating tailored pathways for students and professionals, ensuring that they have the critical thinking skills and technical expertise required in today's evolving global economy. Our commitment to educational equity drives everything we do.
